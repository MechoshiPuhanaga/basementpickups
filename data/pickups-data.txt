WHITE PEARL — STRAT-STYLE HUMBUCKER SET

Neck Pickup
• Magnet: Alnico 3
• DCR: 5.9kΩ
• Inductance: 2.8H
• Self-Resonant Peak: 8.4 kHz
• Loaded Resonant Peak: 3.9 kHz

Description:
The White Pearl neck pickup was designed for players seeking maximum clarity, openness, and articulation from a humbucker-equipped Strat-style guitar. Its relatively low inductance and high resonant frequency create an airy top end with excellent note separation and dynamic response. Clean tones remain sparkling and detailed, while overdriven sounds reveal complex harmonic content without becoming harsh. The bass response is intentionally controlled and tight, avoiding the excessive boominess often associated with neck humbuckers. The result is a balanced, musical voice that responds beautifully to picking dynamics and volume control adjustments.

Bridge Pickup
• Magnet: Alnico 4
• DCR: 6.8kΩ
• Inductance: 3.5H
• Self-Resonant Peak: 7.4 kHz
• Loaded Resonant Peak: 3.4 kHz

Description:
The White Pearl bridge pickup complements the neck with additional authority, harmonic richness, and sustain while preserving the set's overall clarity. It delivers articulate lows, detailed mids, and smooth highs that cut through a mix without sounding aggressive. Rich overtones emerge naturally when driven, making it equally suitable for expressive lead work and rhythm playing. Its balanced frequency response and controlled low end make it particularly effective in Strat-style instruments where definition and responsiveness are essential.

TWIN BLISS — DUAL MAGNET PAF HUMBUCKER

Pickup Specifications
• Magnet: Dual Alnico 5 Bar Magnets
• DCR: 7.4kΩ
• Inductance: 4.0H
• Self-Resonant Peak: 7.0 kHz
• Loaded Resonant Peak: 3.2 kHz

Description:
Twin Bliss features an innovative dual-magnet architecture, employing two smaller Alnico 5 magnets positioned beneath the slug and screw rows rather than a single central bar magnet. This design contributes to its exceptionally balanced frequency response and dynamic feel. In the neck position it produces smooth, velvety cleans with remarkable clarity and no muddiness. In the bridge position it delivers articulate vintage tones with tight lows, detailed mids, and sweet highs. Its versatility, responsiveness, and classic PAF character make it an excellent all-purpose humbucker suitable for a wide variety of guitars and musical styles.
